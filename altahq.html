How the Qualification agent works

A simple flow: define your ICP, run qualification at scale, get evidence-backed decisions, and route only the right accounts into the rest of your outbound motion.

1 Define ICP & disqualifiers

Your ICP, enforced (not “remembered”)

Set the rules that matter: who you win with, who you don’t, and the signals that should increase or decrease priority. The agent uses this to make consistent fit calls.

Pairs with your CRM + data sources
Support media: ICP settings UI Swap for real product shot
2 Run qualification on lists or CRM

Qualify hundreds of accounts without the busywork

Apply your ICP to inbound, outbound lists, or existing CRM accounts. Qualification is the gate — so bad-fit accounts don’t consume research, contacts, or plays.

See why it wins Stops wasted effort early
Support media: list → qualified pipeline view Swap for workflow diagram
3 Evidence-backed decisions

“Fit / no fit” with reasons you can trust

Every decision includes rationale — so reps and leaders can understand what drove the call, and refine ICP rules as the motion evolves.

Built for consistency across reps
Support media: “reasons” panel Swap for real UI
4 Route to downstream agents

Only the right accounts move forward

Send qualified accounts to Research & Signals, Contact Finder, Plays, or Account Planning — and push structured outputs back into CRM/sequencers so the team can execute fast.
