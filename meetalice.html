How it works

How Qualification gates pipeline

Simple flow: define the rules → run at scale → route the right accounts downstream.

01

Define ICP rules that actually hold up

  • Set fit criteria, disqualifiers, and tier logic by segment.
  • Standardize what “good” looks like so reps don’t improvise.
  • Make it easy to tighten or expand the ICP as you learn.
Explore GTM Brain
02

Run qualification on lists or CRM views

  • Apply your ICP to target lists, inbound accounts, or your existing CRM.
  • Generate fit + tier outcomes in batches, not one-by-one research.
  • Stop wasted effort before it starts.
Best used as the first gate
03

Route winners into downstream agents

  • Send Tier 1 accounts to Research & Signals and Contact Finder.
  • Push “no fit” out of the rep workflow (and out of sequences).
  • Sync outcomes back to CRM so teams stay aligned.
