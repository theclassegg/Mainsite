How it works

One workflow that fixes the five biggest GTM time sinks.

Generic outbound + scattered tools is a system problem. Evergrowth solves it with an agentic flow: qualify accounts, pull signals, build buying groups, draft plays, and sync outputs into your stack.

Meet the agents
1 Qualification

Block bad fit before reps waste hours.

Every list starts with an evidence-backed fit decision — so your team stops selling to accounts that can’t buy.

  • Clear “pursue / don’t pursue” decision
  • Reasons tied to your ICP (not vague scores)
  • Routing by segment/tier before downstream work
2 Research & Signals

Get the “why now” without tab chaos.

Agents surface signals you can sell into — and package them into a brief reps can use immediately.

  • Source-backed signal summaries
  • Fast account briefs and call prep
  • Run on demand or in batches
3 Contact Finder

Find persona-fit buyers — not random names.

Build buying groups so reps multithread from day one, with reachability-focused enrichment as needed.

  • Persona-fit contacts per account
  • Coverage across decision-makers + influencers
  • Structured output for CRM + sequencers
4 Plays

Draft outreach that sounds prepared, not generic.

Plays are tied to signals, buyer personas, and your messaging — so reps don’t start from a blank page.

  • Openers, emails, talk tracks, follow-ups
  • Persona-aware framing + proof points
  • Designed to drop into sequences
5 Digital Twins

Pressure-test your angle before outreach.

Get guidance on what to lead with, what to avoid, and how to handle likely objections — faster.

  • Angle recommendation per account/persona
  • Objection prep + talk track starters
  • Rep confidence without guesswork
+ Integrations

Keep your stack — just make it work better.

Evergrowth sits upstream, then syncs structured outputs into CRM and your execution tools.

  • CRM-ready fields, notes, and views
  • Sequencer-ready plays and contact sets
  • Fewer tools, fewer gaps, less drift
