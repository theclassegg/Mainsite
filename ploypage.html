The hidden cost

Generic outreach is just the tip of the iceberg.

The real problem is the time sink and inconsistency underneath: reps are researching, guessing fit, hunting contacts, writing from scratch, and using disconnected tools.

  • Outbound: generic copy + low confidence
  • Research: hours of tabs → messy notes
  • Contacts: good-fit buyers are hard to find
  • Fit: reps learn “bad fit” after time is sunk
  • Leadership: outcomes are hard to influence with fragmented tools
